Layer 1 (spineRig1)
Nathan Vegdahl's Torso Rig with no pivot slide.
Pros:
    1 Independent pelvis rotation and twist
    2 Independent spine bend from lumbar spine base 
      - natural pivot point
    3 No implicit translation
Cons:
    1 Cannot change pivot.
    2 Cannot alter curvature of spine.
    3 Rotation of spine base rotates the hips in the
      opposite direction by an equal amount.
---------------------------------------------------
Layer 2 (spineRig2)
Nathan Vegdahl's Torso with Pivot Slide/Reverse Chain Rig.
Intended to be combined with SpineRig1.
Pros:
    1 Independent pelvis rotation and twist
    2 Independent spine bend from lumbar spine base 
      - natural pivot point
    3 No implicit translation
    4 Pivot point can be changed to be anywhere along
      length of the spine+hips
Cons:
    1 Cannot alter curvature of spine without moving the
      of the child bone (counter-animation issue)
    2 Cannot isolate hip rotation
---------------------------------------------------
Layer 3 (spineRig3)
Nathan Vegdahl's complete humanoid bipedal spine rig.
Combination of the previous two rigs.
Pros:
    1 Visually indicate spine pivot as body bone.
Cons:
    1 Cannot un-isolate spine from the hips.
---------------------------------------------------
Layer 4 (spineRig4)
Lee Slavemini's complete humanois bipeddal spine rig. An
extension from Nathan Veghdahl's complete humanoid spine
rig.
Pros:
    1 Switchable parent between spine and pelvis.
Cons:
    1 No visual indicator or spine pivot.
    2 One bone used for thoracic spine, therefore unable
      to get some spine curvatures that invlove deforming
      only the thoracic spine.
---------------------------------------------------
Layer 5 (spineRig5)
Cartoony spline ik rig.
Cons:
    1 Often gets stuck.
    2 Does not update in real time
    3 Probably needs a separate control rig
---------------------------------------------------
Layer 6 (spineRig 6 & spine6Control)
Cartoony spline ik rig.
Cons:
    1 Unstable, glitchy
    2 Unintuitive controls. Scale affects something else.
    3 Needs more than one rig
    4 Twist deformation is unreliable.
